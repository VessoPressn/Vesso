Every morning, billions of hands wrap around a coffee cup. For twelve minutes, nothing else exists — no scroll, no skip, no ad blocker.

Vesso turns that ritual into a precision media channel. We supply branded cups at zero cost to cafés. Advertisers fund everything in exchange for daily, physical, impossible-to-ignore impressions.
